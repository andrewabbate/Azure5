The Schoenfeld's

We're an American family of three living in Luxembourg, squished in between France, Beligium, and Germany.  Sort of a strange place.  The best way I can describe it is, "The Truman Show". A place where everything is so quaint and charming that it ceases to be quaint and charming.  I suppose the grass is always greener over there, but I can't help but miss the grit of a city like London or New York.
