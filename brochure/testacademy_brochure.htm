Why Is TestAcademy So Easy To Use?
 We employ the Training Knowledge Base (TKB) technology, which offers our instructors:

  1. Familiar editing tools to author questions and tests. Instructors can use toolbar buttons to change fonts, text styles and colors, bullets, and numbered lists.
  2. A Windows Explorer-like environment to manage and publish their classes.
  3. Folders and question libraries to organize their class materials.
  4. The ability to share class materials with other instructors.
  5. A design that minimizes the reliance on varying Internet performance.
